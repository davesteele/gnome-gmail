GNOME Gmail Privacy Policy.

Viagee complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy. GNOME Gmail requests the Gmail "compose" permissions, and stores tokens required to authenticate against the Gmail API. These tokens are stored in the GNOME Keyring. It also stores the last-used sender email address, to offer as a default for future use.
